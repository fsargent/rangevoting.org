About the Range Voting Bulletin Board, or "Blog"

Joining the RV blog allows you to read, post, and text-search our messages, with optional email auto-delivery to you. Can withdraw at any time. Can then participate in our polls, etc. Joining the Bulletin Board resembles subscribing to a newspaper in that it does not signify your endorsement of anything.

If you want to join, click the above link to take you to the bulletin board's front page, then click "join" or "new user sign up" and follow subsequent instructions.
