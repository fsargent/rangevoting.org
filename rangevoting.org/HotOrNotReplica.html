HOT or NOT

This is an approximate visual replica of hotornot.com page as it used to appear, before the site was radically altered by a new owner sometime around 2011. Shown for illustrative purposes only, actual functionality removed. The real site also had menus allowing you to check only women, only men, or both, and/or with any of 4 possible age-ranges. You could submit your own picture to be rated, usually by over 1000 people. (Buttons were also added later permitting communication, arranging meets, etc.)

As soon as you clicked a hotness-rating such as "7" it would instantly tell you what the average-hotness rating was for that picture, then send you to a new picture of somebody else. You could score pictures at a tremendous rate and this site indeed collected more "votes" than all elections ever held in human history.
