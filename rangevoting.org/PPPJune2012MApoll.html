27 June 2012 Poll Watch: PPP (D) Massachusetts 2012 Presidential Survey

Note this poll was of Massachusetts only.

If the candidates for President this year were Democrat Barack Obama, Republican Mitt Romney, Libertarian Gary Johnson, and Green Party candidate Jill Stein, who would you vote for?

  • Barack Obama 53%
  • Mitt Romney 38%
  • Jill Stein 3%
  • Gary Johnson 1%
  • Not sure 4%

Do you have a favorable or unfavorable opinion of Gary Johnson?

  • Favorable 5%
  • Unfavorable 14%

Do you have a favorable or unfavorable opinion of Jill Stein?

  • Favorable 12%
  • Unfavorable 23%

Do you approve or disapprove of Barack Obama's job performance?

  • Approve 53%
  • Disapprove 43%

Regardless of how you feel about him now, do you approve or disapprove of the work Mitt Romney did while he was Governor of Massachusetts?

  • Approve 40%
  • Disapprove 46%

Survey of 902 Massachusetts voters was conducted June 22-24, 2012. The margin of error is ±3.3 percentage points.
Data compilation and analysis courtesy of The Argo Journal
