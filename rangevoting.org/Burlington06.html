Burlington Vermont 2006 IRV mayor election

Official Burlington Mayoral 2006 IRV race results from http://www.burlingtonvotes.org/20060307/. 9788 ballots of which 77 were declared "invalid" before counting began. Ploof, Louie the Cowman, Curley, and the WriteIns were eliminated immediately & simultaneously since their "defeat was mathematically inevitable." That left Miller vs Kiss in the final round, which was won by Kiss.
