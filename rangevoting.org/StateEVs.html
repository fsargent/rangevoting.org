Populations from the USA Census as of 1 July 2003; Electoral Votes from the Federal Elections Commission.

State Populations vs. Electoral Votes

A state's number of electoral votes is its number of senators (which always is 2) plus its number of congressmen. For example, a state with 7 EVs has 5 congressmen.
