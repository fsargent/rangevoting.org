Australia 2001 House Elections (held saturday 10 Nov)
================Warren D. Smith and Malcolm MacKerras============

150 total seats, IRV used to elect each.  
Turnout = 94.85% (natl average).

At the November 2001 Australian general election there were five "flip" results. They were in 
Cowper and Paterson in New South Wales, Chisholm in Victoria, Brisbane in Queensland and 
Hasluck in Western Australia.

The two top parties are the "National/Liberal/CLP" party (really effectively only 1 party but
since, historically, formed from smaller parties merging, seems like more; I suppose it is 
technically possible for all "three" of these parties to run candidates against the others but 
that is very rare, in fact I do not think it occurred) -- and Labor=ALP.

The winners were as follows:
LABOR:           65 seats
NatLibCLP:       82 seats
INDependent:      3 seats
"third parties":  0 seats
-------------------------
TOTAL:          150 seats
