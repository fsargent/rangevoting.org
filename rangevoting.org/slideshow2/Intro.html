Range Voting Slide Show

This is the start page for a "slide show" about range voting. There is an accompanying audio narration. The whole show takes about 60?? minutes. The slides will auto-advance for you, although you always can speed things up by clicking ">>" to cut short the speech and move forward. You can also hit your browser's "back" button to return to a slide that went by too quickly (but that does not work in Mozilla, due to a bug; I will eventually build a workaround by creating my own bac button "<<" but those buttons currently disfunctional), or click "return to start" to come back here.

You now should be hearing some music ♫♪♬. If not, adjust your speakers & sound system stuff until you are hearing it. (It is not essential to have sound since you can watch the slide show in "silent movie" mode – but it's not as fun.) Also, please maximize the size of your browser window on your screen.

It also helps if your browser knows about "unicode" extended character sets. There should be about 140 interesting symbols inside this box. If too many are missing, load more fonts (IE, mozilla) and play with browser settings until the missing ones appear. (Again, unicode is not essential, but your viewing experience will be less fun without it.)
