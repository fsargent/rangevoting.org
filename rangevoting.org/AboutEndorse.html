About Endorsing CRV goals

CRV cannot change the world unless the right people listen to our ideas. They won't do that unless they can see our ideas already have a lot of support. So we need a lot of endorsements. We want over 1000 endorsers. If you click the Endorse link, you will be taken to a page containing a number of statements you can choose to endorse or not (independent choice for each). You then will be asked to fill out a form – and you will not need to reveal private information beyond your name and regional-location – and presto, your name will be glorified by addition to the endorsers list.
